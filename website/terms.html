1. Service Description

ChainRPC ("the Service," "ChainRPC," "we," "us," or "our") provides multi-chain JSON-RPC endpoint access for blockchain networks. The Service proxies standard Ethereum-compatible JSON-RPC requests to blockchain full nodes operated by or on behalf of ChainRPC.

The Service is designed for software developers, system integrators, and technical operators who require reliable, low-latency access to blockchain network data for the purpose of building, testing, and operating decentralized applications, infrastructure tooling, monitoring systems, and similar technical projects.

Supported networks include, but are not limited to: Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum One, Optimism, Polygon PoS, BNB Chain, Avalanche, Gnosis, zkSync Era, Linea, Scroll, Blast, Mantle, Polygon zkEVM, Mode, Zora, Celo, Ethereum Sepolia, and Base Sepolia. The list of supported networks may change at any time without notice.

The Service operates on a best-effort basis. It is not a financial service, payment processor, custodian, or exchange. ChainRPC does not transmit, hold, or settle funds on behalf of any user.

4. Service Tiers and Rate Limits

4.1 Free Tier

The Free tier provides 25,000 requests per day across all supported chains, with a rate limit of 3 requests per second. The Free tier is subject to a hard daily limit — requests exceeding the daily quota receive an HTTP 429 response until the quota resets at midnight UTC. The Free tier is provided as-is and may be modified or discontinued at any time.

4.2 Growth Tier

The Growth tier provides 500,000 requests per day, with a rate limit of 30 requests per second, plus overflow at $10.00 USD per additional 1,000,000 requests. Overflow is charged from the user's approved USDC or USDT balance as it accrues.

4.3 Pro Tier

The Pro tier provides 5,000,000 requests per day, with a rate limit of 100 requests per second, plus overflow at $5.00 USD per additional 1,000,000 requests. Overflow is charged from the user's approved USDC or USDT balance as it accrues.

4.4 Changes to Tiers

ChainRPC reserves the right to modify the limits, prices, features, and availability of any tier at any time. Existing paid users will receive at least 14 days' notice before any price increase takes effect. Tier changes that reduce included quota without a corresponding price reduction will be subject to the same 14-day notice.

4.5 Rate Limit Enforcement

All tiers are subject to per-second burst limits. Exceeding the per-second limit will result in HTTP 429 responses regardless of remaining daily quota. Persistent burst abuse may result in temporary IP-level throttling or key suspension.

5. Payment, Billing, and Receipts

5.1 Payment Method

Paid tiers are billed in USDC (USD Coin) or USDT (Tether USD) on the Base network (chain ID 8453). No other payment methods are accepted. ChainRPC does not accept credit cards, bank transfers, checks, cash, or any other form of payment.

5.2 Wallet Linkage

To upgrade to a paid tier, you must link an Ethereum-compatible wallet address to your API key via your account page. You must then approve ChainRPC's billing contract to pull from your USDC or USDT balance. The approval amount represents your maximum authorized spend. ChainRPC will never pull more than the approved allowance.

5.3 Billing Mechanism

ChainRPC operates on a pull-based billing model. Your USDC or USDT remains in your wallet at all times. ChainRPC pulls the monthly subscription fee at the start of each billing cycle and pulls overflow charges as they are incurred. You retain full custody of your funds until a pull occurs. You may reduce or revoke the approval at any time via your wallet to prevent future pulls.

5.4 Receipts

All payments are settled on-chain. Every pull generates an on-chain transaction that serves as your receipt. You may retrieve transaction records at any time from your account page or directly from the Base network block explorer. ChainRPC does not issue paper invoices, PDF invoices, or email receipts. The on-chain transaction is your receipt.

5.5 Billing Failures

If a billing pull fails — due to insufficient USDC/USDT balance, revoked approval, or any other reason — your key will be downgraded to the Free tier at the start of the next billing cycle. No debt is carried. No collection action will be taken. No penalty fee will be applied. You may re-upgrade at any time.

5.6 Overflow Billing

Overflow charges are calculated per million requests beyond your tier's daily included limit and pulled from your approved balance. If your balance is insufficient to cover an overflow charge, overflow billing stops and requests above the included limit receive HTTP 429 responses until the balance is replenished or the daily quota resets.
